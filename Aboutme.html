About me

Hi! My name is Susan Franzen, I studies Ancient Cultures between 2004 and 2011 at the Vrije Universiteit in Amsterdam. I wrote both my BA and MA theses on palaces; my BA on the difference in decoration between the Persian and Neo-Assyrian palaces, my MA thesis on the difference in decoration between several Neo-Assyrian palaces.

Why this website?

When I decided I wanted to start learning about web development, I realized I needed a website project that I found interesting enough to keep make an entire website around and that I could find texts and images for relatively fast; what better subject than the palaces of the Ancient World, which I have never lost my fascination for? The idea is to keep expanding this page, to keep practicing. Stay tuned!
